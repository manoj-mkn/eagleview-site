Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how NewMan Tech ("we", "our", or "us"), a technology company based in India, handles information in connection with the Eagle View desktop application ("Application", "Service").

Eagle View is a productivity widget that connects to your Microsoft Outlook or Google Gmail account to display a real-time summary of emails, calendar meetings, and flagged tasks — all processed locally on your device.

This Policy is designed to meet the requirements of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act), and internationally recognised privacy principles including OECD Privacy Guidelines and ISO/IEC 29101.

Plain-language summary: Eagle View reads data from your email and calendar providers using your own credentials. This data is shown to you inside the app. It is never sent to our servers. We do not sell, share, or monetise your personal data.
2

Data We Access

Eagle View requests the following permissions from your connected accounts. All access is read-only. We never write, modify, or delete data in your accounts.

Data Category Specific Fields Accessed Purpose Stored Locally?
Email metadata Sender name, subject line, received time, unread count, flag status Display unread count and flagged mail list In-memory only; not persisted to disk
Calendar events Event title, start/end time, meeting join URL, attendee list Display today's upcoming meetings In-memory only; not persisted to disk
Authentication tokens OAuth 2.0 access token, refresh token, expiry timestamp Maintain authenticated session without repeated login Yes — encrypted in local app settings file
App settings Refresh intervals, widget size/position, notes, reminder data Restore user preferences across app restarts Yes — local settings file on your device
Email content is never accessed. Eagle View reads only metadata (sender, subject, time). The body of your emails is not read, stored, or transmitted under any circumstances.

Local Storage & Security

All data handled by Eagle View resides exclusively on your local device. The following file is created during normal operation:

FileLocationContents
eagle-view-settings.json macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/com.newmantech.eagleview/
Windows: %APPDATA%\com.newmantech.eagleview\		 OAuth tokens (encrypted at rest), widget preferences, notes, reminder data

Security Measures

  • OAuth tokens are stored using the platform's native secure storage primitives
  • All API communication uses TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS only)
  • The Application requests minimum necessary OAuth scopes (principle of least privilege)
  • No telemetry, crash reporting, or usage analytics data is transmitted from the Application
  • The Application's Rust backend enforces memory safety to prevent buffer overflow and injection attacks
You are the data controller. Because all data stays on your device, you retain full control. You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the settings file above or uninstalling the Application.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. Because Eagle View does not transmit data to our servers, most of these rights can be exercised directly by you within the Application or your operating system.

Rights under GDPR GDPR

Right of Access (Art. 15)

You can view all data the app accesses directly within Eagle View's interface.

Right to Erasure (Art. 17)

Log out and delete the settings file to erase all locally stored data.

Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18)

Disconnect your account via Settings → Log Out to stop all data processing.

Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

Your settings file is a standard JSON file you may copy or transfer freely.

Right to Object (Art. 21)

Uninstall the Application to cease all data processing immediately.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

Contact your national supervisory authority (e.g., ICO, CNIL, BfDI).

Rights under CCPA/CPRA CCPA

International Data Transfers

NewMan Tech is headquartered in India. Because Eagle View processes all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to our servers, there are no international transfers of personal data by NewMan Tech.

When Eagle View connects to Microsoft or Google APIs on your behalf, your data may be processed on servers operated by these providers in various countries. These transfers are governed by Microsoft's and Google's respective data transfer mechanisms (including Standard Contractual Clauses under GDPR).

For enterprise deployments subject to data residency requirements, please ensure your Microsoft or Google tenant is configured with the appropriate regional data boundary settings before deploying Eagle View.
