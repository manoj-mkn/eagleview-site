Features

nothing you don't

All your work tools in one slim sidebar. Always visible, never in the way.

📧

Unified Mail

Connects to both Outlook and Gmail. See unread count, today's mails, and flagged items — all from one place.

Outlook + Gmail
📅

Live Meetings

See today's calendar events with real-time countdown. One-click join for Teams and Google Meet calls.

● Live countdown

Jira Issues

Track assigned Jira tickets with status pills. Update status transitions directly from the widget — no browser needed.

Status transitions

Smart Notes

Inline notepad with drag reorder, reminder bells, and checkbox completion. Your to-do list is always visible.

With reminders
